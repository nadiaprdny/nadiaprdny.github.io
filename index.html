Brooklyn Nine-Nine

The series was so finely written, it has bring a lot of emotions to the viewers. this TV show has everything! The drama, the friendship, the love! Whatever Gina Linetti is feeling, we all feel it too. The plot is unexpected like Captain Holt's expression.
I think we can all agree that our favorite series are the Halloween Heists, so festive, so alive. i think that's what makes halloween fun. I can't decide which one is my favorite comfort Episode of all time but it has gotta be either the "Jake & Amy" Season 5 Episode 22 where they got married (never fails fo make me cry btw) Or, the first ever halloween heist! Modern Family

Brilliant, Astonishing, Amazing, Greatest work of all time. I think that described it enough.
I kid. But in all seriousness, it's perfect. Cam and Mitch doesn't go a day without the drama. Jay and Gloria without Stella and Phil and Claire without yelling at their kids! It shocked me when they said it was filmed on a set and not in the ACTUAL house. It feels so homey just by looking through the screen. My favorite character has got to be Mitch, i relate to him so much.
The boundary issues, the "my soulmate is a clown" and the fact that he's a lawyer (my dream job) And the fact that Eric Stonestreet is a straight man is shocking to me! He killed it as Cam! But oh? It's Sofia Vergara walking to the set. All eyes on her!

Gilmore Girls

Supercharged, sarcastic and comical, Gilmore Girls have been a delight throughout the original and revival series. Although musicals were redundant and certain stories had loose ends in the revival, especially that of Rory's, the state of the series remain intact in terms of tone and dialogue.
For those who disliked the revival, it might be easy to look at the 4 feature-length episodes as an alternate ending to the original that serves the purpose of reflection rather than progression. I'm on #TeamLogan he's Mr.Perfect don't you think?
